Repair Cases

Real results from our recent diagnostics and repair work

Suspension Alignment Correction Case

Vehicle: BMW 3 Series Duration: 4 hours

Customer reported uneven tyre wear and vehicle pulling to one side. Upon inspection, we discovered misaligned suspension geometry and worn suspension components affecting the vehicle's handling and safety.

Problems Identified:

  • Front camber out of specification
  • Toe angles incorrect on rear axle
  • Worn control arm bushings
  • Uneven tyre wear pattern

Work Completed:

  • Replaced worn control arm bushings
  • Performed four-wheel alignment using laser equipment
  • Adjusted camber, caster, and toe angles to manufacturer specifications
  • Road test to verify handling improvements

Result:

Vehicle now tracks straight with improved handling and tyre longevity. Customer reported significantly better driving experience and even tyre wear after the repair.

Full Engine Diagnostics Repair Case

Vehicle: Audi A6 Duration: 6 hours

Vehicle arrived with check engine light on, rough idle, and significant loss of power. Customer had visited another garage without success. We performed comprehensive diagnostics to identify the root cause.

Diagnostic Process:

  • Connected advanced OBD-II scanner
  • Retrieved multiple fault codes
  • Performed compression test
  • Inspected fuel pressure and injectors
  • Tested ignition system components

Issues Found:

  • Faulty mass airflow sensor providing incorrect readings
  • Carbon buildup on intake valves
  • One ignition coil failing intermittently

Repairs Made:

  • Replaced mass airflow sensor with OEM part
  • Performed intake valve carbon cleaning
  • Replaced faulty ignition coil and spark plugs
  • Cleared fault codes and verified all systems operating correctly

Result:

Engine now runs smoothly with full power restored. No check engine light, improved fuel economy, and customer reported the car drives like new again.

Complete Brake System Overhaul

Vehicle: Mercedes-Benz C-Class Duration: 5 hours

Customer experienced squealing brakes, pulsating brake pedal, and extended stopping distances. Safety inspection revealed worn brake components throughout the system requiring immediate attention.

Inspection Findings:

  • Front brake pads worn to minimum thickness
  • Warped front brake discs
  • Rear brake pads at 20% remaining
  • Contaminated brake fluid
  • Sticking rear caliper

Work Performed:

  • Replaced all four brake discs and pads
  • Serviced and lubricated calipers
  • Replaced sticking rear caliper
  • Performed complete brake fluid flush
  • Road test and brake performance verification

Result:

Braking system restored to optimal performance with smooth, quiet operation and confident stopping power. Customer peace of mind restored with comprehensive warranty coverage.

Air Conditioning System Repair

Vehicle: Volkswagen Golf Duration: 3 hours

AC system blowing warm air despite recent recharge at another facility. Customer frustrated with recurring issue and seeking permanent solution.

Diagnostic Approach:

  • Pressure tested AC system for leaks
  • UV dye trace to locate leak source
  • Compressor operation test
  • Electrical system diagnostics

Problem Discovered:

  • Condenser leak due to stone damage
  • Low refrigerant levels
  • Contaminated refrigerant oil

Repairs Completed:

  • Replaced damaged condenser
  • Flushed AC system
  • Evacuated and recharged with correct refrigerant amount
  • Added UV dye for future leak detection
  • Replaced cabin air filter

Result:

AC system now operating at optimal efficiency with ice-cold air output. No leaks detected, system holding pressure perfectly. Customer delighted with proper diagnosis and permanent fix.
