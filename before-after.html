Real transformations from our repair and restoration work

Before After

Paint Correction & Restoration

This vehicle came in with severe scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation from years of exposure. Using our multi-stage paint correction process, we removed surface defects, restored the original paint depth, and applied a protective ceramic coating. The result is a mirror-like finish that looks better than new.

Process included: Deep cleaning, clay bar treatment, compound polishing, fine polishing, and ceramic coating application.

Headlight Restoration

Oxidised and yellowed headlights not only look bad but also reduce nighttime visibility and safety. This restoration removed all oxidation layers, polished the lens surface, and applied a UV-protective clear coat to prevent future damage.

Benefits: Improved visibility, enhanced appearance, and passed MOT inspection requirements.

Complete Interior Detailing

A thorough interior transformation including deep cleaning of all surfaces, upholstery shampooing, leather conditioning, and odour elimination. Every crevice was cleaned, and protective treatments were applied to preserve the interior.

Included: Vacuum and extraction, leather treatment, dashboard restoration, carpet shampooing, and air freshening.

Alloy Wheel Refurbishment

Scratched and kerbed alloy wheels can dramatically affect your vehicle's appearance. Our refurbishment process includes chemical stripping, surface repair, powder coating, and clear coat protection for a factory-fresh finish.

Repairs: Kerb damage repair, surface levelling, powder coating, and diamond cutting where applicable.
